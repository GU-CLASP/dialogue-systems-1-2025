Guess the Intruder

In this game, you'll have the chance to interrogate 6 people that are now in the same place about their role in this context. Your goal is to guess which person does not belong in the context. You can ask each person multiple questions, that will be answered with a variation of yes/no answers. Whenever you want to switch to ask the next person, just say: "next". Try to keep your questions the least personal as possible. For example, don't ask questions such as 'Do you like what you're doing?'; But you can ask if what they're doing is stressful or if where they are is a happy place or not. If you want some inspiration for what to ask, press the help button.

Good luck and enjoy!
